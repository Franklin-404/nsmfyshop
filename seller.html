How Payments Work (Escrow)

nsmfy uses a safe payment (escrow) system. Here's how it works:

  1. Buyer pays nsmfy — funds are held securely, not sent to you yet.
  2. You ship the item — enter your tracking number below once dispatched.
  3. Buyer confirms receipt — they confirm they received the item in good condition.
  4. Funds released to you — payment is transferred to your account within 1-2 business days.

This protects both you and your buyer from scams.
